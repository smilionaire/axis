Axis International English Boarding Secondary School is located in NamunaPath, Butwal-10, Sukhkhanagar, Rupandehi . This secondary school is affiliated with National Examination Board (NEB) and offers plus two (10+2) programs. Butwal is set to be an educational hub in Rupandehi district. Literacy rate in the city is considered to be high. It has held the record for Nepal Top students in SLC as well as HSEB examination numerous times.
